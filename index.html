About

Aeravat is a National Level AI Hackathon Conducted by the IEEE Bombay Section Computer Society, hosted by the IEEE CS Student Chapter, SPIT in association with various institutes across Mumbai and Maharashtra.

Domains

Machine Learning

Participants in the Machine Learning domain are challenged to create innovative solutions leveraging machine learning algorithms, addressing real-world problems and pushing the boundaries of data-driven decision-making.

Generative AI

Dive into the Generative AI domain where participants explore cutting-edge techniques to generate novel content, whether it's art, music, or other creative outputs, showcasing the power of artificial intelligence in fostering creativity and expression.

Security and AI

Participants in the AI for Security domain are tasked with developing intelligent solutions to enhance cybersecurity, leveraging artificial intelligence to detect and mitigate threats, safeguarding digital ecosystems and critical infrastructure.

AI for social cause

Engage in the AI for Social Cause domain to design impactful artificial intelligence solutions that contribute to societal well-being, addressing critical issues such as healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.

Guidelines

  • Round 1: Abstract Submission
    • This round requires the teams to submit the abstract of their solution to the given problem statement
    • 40 teams will be Shortlisted in this round.
    • This round is free for all.
    • The length of the abstract should be between 200-500 words.
    • No more than 10% Plagiarism will be allowed.
  • Round 2: Offline Hackathon.
    • Shortlisted teams will be invited for 24-hour Offline Hackathon will be held on 22-23 March at SPIT Mumbai.
    • Teams selected for this round would have to pay a registration fee as mentioned below.
  • Payments (For Round 2 participants)
    • Non- IEEE Members - Rs.500
    • IEEE Members - Rs. 300
    • IEEE CS Members - Rs.200

FAQs

When will the problem statements be released?

+ -

Problem Statements would be released tentatively on 20th of March

Does the project need to be submitted before presentation round?

+ -

In round one, the abstract of another problem statement(mentioned in the unstop link) has to be submitted. This is just a description of the solution and not the actual project. The problem statements for the hackathon would be different.

What if I have never been in a hackathon before?

+ -

Don't worry, there's always a first time. Aeravat provides first-timers a great opportunity to collaborate, connect, learn and grow. And if you have the skills, you can even win in your first Hackathon!

Will my team be able to call on mentors for help?

+ -

Mentors will be available at the venue to provide guidance.
